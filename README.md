## 🌿 About

**Ella Homestay** is a fully self-contained homestay nestled in the misty hills of **Kuttikanam, Kerala** — between Wagamon and Peermade at 900m altitude.

When you book, you get the **entire home** — not individual rooms. 3 bedrooms, a full kitchen, garden, and private compound. No strangers. No shared spaces. Just your people and the hills.
